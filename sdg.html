What is Gender Equality?

Gender equality is the idea that all people, regardless of gender, have the same rights, opportunities, and access to resources. It also means that the needs, aspirations, and behaviors of all people are valued equally. Gender equality can also refer to equality in relation to sexual orientation and gender identity.
