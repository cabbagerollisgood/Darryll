It's Me Hi!

Aspiring Artist

Gender equality has been a dream of mine, something I have always wanted to see become a reality. I believe everyone deserves the right to respect and the same privileges that others receive. In the past, things were much worse, many people suffered, and some even lost their lives due to discrimination based on their gender. However, as time goes on, the situation has improved slightly. I hope that by raising awareness and educating people about gender issues, they can become more open-minded and respectful towards others.
